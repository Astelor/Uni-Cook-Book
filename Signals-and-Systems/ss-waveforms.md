> aka rectangle function, Heaviside function.

> aka triangle function.
> The professor calls it triangle wave, and the wiki says otherwise. I'll write what I found to be "correct".

$$tri(\frac{t-t_0}{T})=Λ(\frac{t-t_0}{T})$$
